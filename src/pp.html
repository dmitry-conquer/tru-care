Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for https://www.trucarehomecare.com/. This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this web site. It will notify you of the following:

  • What data specific to you is being collected from you through our web site, if it is being utilized, and with whom it may be transmitted if at all.
  • What control you have over the data.
  • The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.
  • How you can correct any information, you feel to be incorrect.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

The data collected on our site is the sole property of TruCare Home Care. The only information kept is that at which you voluntarily offer in any correspondence or direct interaction. All data is kept in house and is under no circumstances redistributed to 3rd parties for compensation.

Your information is better at serving your needs and responding accurately to those needs. The only instances where personal information is shared are instances that require 3rd party involvement in order to fulfill your request or order.

Unless you submit a written, digital request, or give us a phone call we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about our specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may opt-out of any future contacts from us at any time by contacting us via the email address, or phone number given on our website:

  • Acquire all personal data set applicable to you
  • Alter any data pertaining to you that you require altering
  • Have us delete any data we have relating to you.
  • Voice and concerns you have about the mining of your data.

Security

It is part of our company’s policy and our highest priority to never allow a data breach on or offline due to negligence.

All payment information is encrypted and exchanged via encryption.

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. All payment processing is handled in a compartmentalized fashion and is not handled by non-essential employees (for example, billing or customer service). In addition, we keep our computer systems and servers in which we store personally identifiable information in a secure environment.

Updates

Our privacy policy is subject to change. any information relating to these changes can be found on our website

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via telephone at 833-878-2273 or via email.

Cookies

We use “cookies” on this site. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site visitor’s hard drive to help us improve your access to our site and identify repeat visitors to our site. For instance, when we use a cookie to identify you, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our site.
